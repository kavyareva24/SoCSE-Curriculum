**COURSE OVERVIEW:**

This course introduces students to the fundamentals of Cyber Forensics, emphasizing the processes involved in identifying, collecting, analyzing, and preserving digital evidence in cybercrime investigations. It equips learners with knowledge of digital crime, forensic tools, legal frameworks, and investigative procedures essential for dealing with digital evidence in various domains such as cyber law enforcement, corporate security, and legal proceedings.

### **COURSE OBJECTIVES (COs):**

The objectives of this course are to:

- Introduce the concepts of cybercrime and cyber forensics.
- Understand the techniques used for digital evidence collection and preservation.
- Study forensic tools and procedures for disk, network, and mobile forensics.
- Familiarize with legal aspects and investigative processes in cybercrime handling.
